狄利克雷函数: \( D(x) = \begin{cases} 1, & x\in\mathbb{Q} \\ 0, & x\notin\mathbb{Q} \end{cases} \)
勒贝格测度: \( m^*(E) = \inf\left\{\sum_{n=1}^\infty |I_n| \mid E\subset\bigcup_{n=1}^\infty I_n\right\} \)
可测集: \( m^*(T)=m^*(T\cap E)+m^*(T\cap E^c), \forall T\subset\mathbb{R}^n \)
Lebesgue积分: \( \int_E f d\mu = \sup\left\{\int_E \phi d\mu \mid 0\leq\phi\leq f, \phi\text{简单函数}\right\} \)
单调收敛定理: \( \int f_n d\mu \uparrow \int f d\mu, f_n\uparrow f \)
控制收敛定理: \( \int f_n d\mu \to \int f d\mu, |f_n|\leq g\in L^1 \)
Fatou引理: \( \int \liminf_{n\to\infty} f_n d\mu \leq \liminf_{n\to\infty} \int f_n d\mu \)
Fubini定理: \( \int_{X\times Y} f d(\mu\times\nu) = \int_X \left(\int_Y f d\nu\right) d\mu \)
Lᵖ空间: \( L^p(\mu) = \{f \mid \int |f|^p d\mu < \infty\}, 1\leq p<\infty \)
Hölder不等式: \( \int |fg| d\mu \leq \|f\|_p \|g\|_q, \frac{1}{p}+\frac{1}{q}=1 \)
Minkowski不等式: \( \|f+g\|_p \leq \|f\|_p + \|g\|_p, 1\leq p<\infty \)
Radon-Nikodym定理: \( \nu \ll \mu \iff \exists f\in L^1, \nu(E)=\int_E f d\mu \)
